What To Do

Enjoy the history of Juneau but leave plenty of time for adventure! The landscape is undeniably beautiful and there are many opportunities to take in the scenery. Complete your day with dinner in the many restaurants in the beautiful, historic downtown and stop in one of the many shops on your way back home.

Shrine of St Theresa
Free will donation, Late hours in the summer months, Amazing wedding location, Cabin rentals
Mendenhall Glacier
Mendenhall Glacier Ice Tours, Mendenhall Glacier Canoe Tours, Dog Sled & Helicopter Adventure, Icefield Helicopter Tours
Historic Downtown Shopping
Variety of delicious restaurants, Jewelry shops, Souvenier shops, Museums
