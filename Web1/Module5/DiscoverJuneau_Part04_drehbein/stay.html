Lodging in Juneau

Part of the traveling experience is finding good lodging! Finding this will take some research, but it can be done. Juneau had some unique lodging opportunities in the historic downtown area including the famous Baranof Hotel, and there are VRBOand Airbnb rentals in Juneau and across the channel on Douglas Island. Douglas is quickly accessible to Juneau and all the exciting adventures of the area.
