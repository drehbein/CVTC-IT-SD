The Franco-Prussian War

1870 was a disaster for France causing that action to thirst for a chance to avenge their territorial losses. On the other side of the hill, the war led to the establishment of a united Gernam enpire headed by Prussia.

History of the War

This war had namy close-run battles that ended poorly for the French. It led indirectly to WWI, which of course, led to WWII.

Why 1870?

Large, colorfully uniformed armies with diverse types of units.


Large-scale battles, many of which were very close-run affairs with lots of "what-if" scenarios.


The fall of one empire. The rise of another.


Seeds sown of a future, yet even more terrible war.
