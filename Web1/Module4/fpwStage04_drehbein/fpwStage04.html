Why 1870?

Large, colorfully uniformed armies with diverse types of units.

Large-scale battles, many of which were very close-run affairs with lots of "what-if" scenarios.

The fall of one empire. The rise of another.

Seeds sown of a future, yet even more terrible war.


Today's Snapshot

"General Frossard, undefeated, thought he had been defeated, and so he was. General von Zastrow was half-defeated, but refused to be so was not. This was the secret to the Prussian victory. "
        - French historian
